Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do I need prior experience to join?

+

No, we welcome all skill levels from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Our instructors will tailor the training to your level.

What should I bring to my first class?

+

Comfortable workout clothes, water bottle, and a positive attitude! Equipment can be rented or purchased at the gym.

Are trial classes available?

+

Yes! We offer a free trial class for all new members. Contact us to schedule your trial session.

What are the membership options?

+
