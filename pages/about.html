About DoBu Martial Arts

Excellence in martial arts training since 2010

Our Story

DoBu Martial Arts was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide world-class martial arts training in a supportive, inclusive environment.

What started as a small dojo with just 15 students has grown into a premier martial arts academy, serving over 500+ active members across all age groups and skill levels.

Our founder, Mauricio Gomez, brought together decades of experience across multiple martial arts disciplines to create a unique training philosophy that blends traditional Eastern martial arts with modern training techniques, emphasizing not just physical prowess, but mental discipline, respect, and personal growth.

Today, DoBu Martial Arts stands as a testament to the transformative power of martial arts, having helped thousands of students achieve their goals, whether that's competing at the highest levels, improving fitness, or building confidence.

Our Journey

Major milestones in the DoBu story

2010

The Beginning

DoBu Martial Arts opened its doors with Mauricio Gomez as head instructor, offering classes in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Muay Thai to 15 founding members.

2013

Expansion

Moved to a larger facility with dedicated training areas for striking, grappling, and weapons. Added Krav Maga and Taekwondo to our class offerings.

2016

Competition Success

DoBu students won their first national championship titles in both BJJ and Muay Thai, establishing our reputation for competitive excellence.

2019

Youth Program Launch

Introduced specialized programs for children and teens, with age-appropriate curriculum focusing on character development and anti-bullying.

2021

Facility Upgrade

Complete renovation with state-of-the-art training equipment, expanded mat space, and dedicated strength & conditioning area.

2024

Today

Serving 500+ members with 15 expert instructors, offering 25+ weekly classes across 8 martial arts disciplines. Recognized as a premier martial arts academy committed to excellence in training.
